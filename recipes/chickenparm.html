Chicken Parm

In this recipe we will be making an italian classic known as the chicken parm. This dish blends a combination of red sauce, chicken, and cheese to create a cripsy and delicous dish. Chicken parm should be made wih love.

Ingredients

  • lb chicken breast
  • canned tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • breadcrumbs and flour

Directions

  1. Start by adding 1 tbsp of oil to a pan and turning the heat to medium/high. Add garlic to the pan and let brown. Add canned tomatoes along with salt and pepper once the garlic browns. Stir occasionaly and then remove from heat and allow to cool.

  2. Butterfly your chicken breast and flatten it until ~1 inch thickness. Coat with a mixture of egg and breadcrumbs

  3. Add 2-3 tbsp of olive oil to a large pan and set heat to medium/high. Cook chicken on one side until brown along the edge and then flip.

  4. Once chicken is cooked put on a sheetpan and top with cheese. Place chicken in broiler until cheese is melted.
